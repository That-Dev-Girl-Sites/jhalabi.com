Respect is a lost art.

A company promises to take care of its employees and treat them as real. In almost the same breath and under the guise of efficiency, they indiscriminently fire teams of people and begin hiring their replacements.

A doctor promises care in treating their patients as people. Their waiting room is packed with people, waiting to see the doctor hours after their appointment.

A friend promises to call. They never call.

Each broken promise is a signal of a lack of respect.
